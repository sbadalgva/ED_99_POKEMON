Betamon

Nacho

A quadrupedal Amphibian Pokemon. Although Betamon has a gentle, docile personality, once it is angered, it emits its "Dengeki Biririn" from its body, generating more than 1 MV of electricity to strike the opponent.

This profile matches its partner in the Digimon Reference Book.
